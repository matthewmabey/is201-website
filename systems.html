Core Information Systems

Companies use different systems for different jobs, but they all connect back to data, processes, and decisions.

Three common ideas

  1. Transaction Processing Systems: handle day to day business activity like orders or payroll.
  2. Management Information Systems: summarize data so managers can track performance.
  3. Decision Support Systems: help people analyze choices and make better calls.
